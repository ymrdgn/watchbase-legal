How to Delete Your WatchBase Account

You can permanently delete your WatchBase account and all associated data directly from within the app.

To do this:

  1. Open the WatchBase app.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Once confirmed, all your account data will be permanently removed from our Supabase database.
